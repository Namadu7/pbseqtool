Move animations start at 117. 
Take a move's index and add 116 to it, 
and that's the file for that move's animation.

Note: while I don't have a list of ORAS BSEQs 
at the time of writing this (2024-11-2), 
I can say that move animations start at 120
in those games. Add 119 to the move's index.

--- means empty sequence.
? means I dunno what it is.
